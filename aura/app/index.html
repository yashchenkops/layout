How it works

The client buys NFTs of different levels. Each level has its own price.
After purchasing the NFT, the client must qualify (bring 2 referrals) to join the queue. The queue can also be purchased for the customer level NFT cost. By bringing 2 referrals, the client receives back 100% of the cost of NFT.

In the queue, the client waits until 2 people stand behind him, after which he will receive 200% of the cost of his level, but 100% will go back to the system in order to re-queue for payment.
Each client has the opportunity to move to a higher level by purchasing the necessary NFT, and repeat the process at a new level.

FAQ

  • How much will a member get for referring new members?

    For the first two referrals, the participant receives 100% from each, starting from the third, 50% for each new participant. The referral program only works to attract new members to the first level.

  • If I buy more NFTs in the same level, will another queue open?

    No. You will simply have more NFTs. One NFT is enough to participate in the program. You can buy NFT as a gift.

  • Can I buy the 5th level immediately without going through the other levels below?

    It will be possible to buy Level 5 NFTs on the secondary market. You can mint NFT by levels strictly sequentially. In order to mint level 5 NFTs, you must have NFTs of the first 4 levels in your wallet.

  • If I buy an NFT from level 2 and give it as a gift, will the new owner be able to take part in level 2 right away with it?

    No. Ownership of NFT tokens of all previous levels will be checked. If the wallet of the new owner contains NFT level 1, he will be able to activate the 2nd level and join the queue.

  • If I join the tier 2 queue and sell my only tier 2 NFT, will I still be in the queue?

    You will remain in line, but you will not receive the money. Your reward will go into the system.

  • Will my place in the queue be sold along with the NFT?

    No.

  • Can I withdraw money from my balance while in the queue?

    You can withdraw all available funds on your balance at any time. Information about your balance and a button for withdrawing all money from it are available in your Personal Account.

  • Can I partially withdraw money?

    No. You can only withdraw the entire amount on your balance.

  • What do I need to do to get in line?

    In the first level, you need to buy a level 1 NFT and earn a qualification. You will automatically be placed in a queue after qualifying.

  • How to get a qualification?

    You may get qualification by referring two new level 1 NFT buyers or doing a turbo boost (buy without attracting buyers at double price of level 1).

  • How to get into the queue of the next level?

    To get to the next level, you need to have passed the qualification in the first level and have the NFT of the previous levels.
