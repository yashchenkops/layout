7. HIGH-ROLLER POLICY

7.1 If the total Net Gaming Revenue for the Customer, in the qualifying month, for a Brand is negative of at least €10 000 or greater, then the High-Roller Policy as set out hereunder, willapply:

7.2. The negative Net Gaming Revenue generated by the High-Roller shall affect Monthly Net Gaming Revenue in the qualifying month, but all commission adjustments are to be made in the subsequent months. The commission of the qualifying month remains unchanged.

7.3 In case of Monthly negative NGR in the qualifying month – the amount has to be carried over to the next periods and deducted from the future Net Gaming Revenue generated by the same High-Rollerbefore the final commission count.

7.4 The negative balance carried over cannot be set-off against other Customers’ future Net Gaming Revenue;

7.5 The negative balance carried over cannot be greater than the total monthly negative Net Gaming Revenue for the particular Brand on the Affiliate account in the qualifying period;

7.6 A positive NGR of a High-Roller in subsequent months will be reduced to nil before the final commission count until the High-Roller's carried over negative NGR is fully refunded.

7.7 A negative balance will not be increased by future negative Net Gaming Revenue unless the High-Roller meets the qualifying criteria in subsequent months.

7.8 If a High-Roller meets the qualifying criteria in subsequent months, a negative balance will be increased in the amount equal to a negative Net Gaming Revenue balance in the qualifying month.

7.9 All positive High-Roller balances under €500 are carried over to the next periods until the amount of €500 and over is accumulated . Once it is reached, the cumulative amount of €500 and over is subtracted from the remaining High-Roller’s negative balance. The commission correction is based on the following formula: Commission = (NGR – Positive HR NGR – 35% Admin Fee) x RS%. In case of any commission correction, all the affiliates should be preliminary informed in details via email from finance@spadespartners.com about the potential commission adjustments. Thus, the invoices must be issued right after the confirmation of commission correction procedure before 15th of each month.
