p.1 The Great Expectations

The Atoms for Peace in the Soviet Union was an idea that united millions. Digital artist Gennady Kozhevny collected a series of USSR posters which had an aim of nuclear propaganda. This NFT collection commemorates the work of Soviet poster artists to reflect the great expectations of that time.

p.2 Liquidators

“Liquidators” is a unique NFT Art collection created by the emerging Ukrainian artist - Viktoriia Marushchak in commemoration of the heroes who gave their lives during the liquidation of the explosion. Where the machines would crash, the humans will succeed by putting their lives at stake.

p.3 The Aftermath

A single art piece that is dedicated to the heartbreaking story of Lyudmila Ignatenko, a wife of a firefighter Vasily Ignatenko who was one of the first victims of the disaster. Viktoria chose the story of Lyudmila as a symbol to illustrate the fate of the people in direct contact with the liquidators.
