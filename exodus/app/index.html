About Exodus

It is a worldwide blockchain ecosystem that brings together several cutting-edge technologies, including NFT and fintech. You are aware that the cryptocurrency business has entered a new phase with endless opportunities for growth with the introduction of non-fungible tokens. As a result, we are combining two massive ecosystems, namely fintech and NFT finance, to develop a new form of blockchain bank system. Earnings for ecosystem referrals are generated using revenues from the sale of NFT collectibles, which in turn are used to support the further growth of the world's first NFT bank. The Exodus algorithm idea is founded on a complex blockchain algorithm. Our ecosystem serves this goal by providing storage for NFTs as well as an exchange for NFTs, which makes it far simpler to use and provides a user-friendly interface.

How the referral system works

The idea is as simple as one can imagine. We are looking to create a strong community of those who adore and live in the NFT space. You are provided with a high-tech finance service with instant cashback available from the very beginning. You will also get 24/7 support starting with this guide about how to engage further in the ecosystem

  • 1. Purchase the exodus pass to activate your adventure with Exodus.
  • 2. After your initial purchase there is a need to refer to your link for an invitation to secure your tree of cashback. You can build limitless branches of cashback in the process by inviting more and more referrals.
  • 3. Your first referral will buy the NFT and you get 20% from their initial purchase when they refer their referral to buy the NFT they get 20% back like you did but also there is a 10% distribution back to you! With the same concept, this works with the next 2 referral levels. When the first referral of the third one in the chain buys the NFT you will get another 5%.
  • 4. There is no limit on how many referrals you can invite on the first level – this means you can build enormous trees of cashback that will reward you daily with passive income! The algorithm of Exodus is secured, transparent, verified, and signed by the smart contract.

Advantages

1. Integration with the Binance Smart Chain

Each user can monitor all activities and transactions without the danger of fraud thanks to the transparency of the system, which is guaranteed by Binance Smart Chain, which also provides extremely cheap user fees.

2. Easy way to return your investment

You could make up to one hundred percent of the level's original purchase price with each cycle, and you wouldn't have to take any risks or put in any work! There are no withdrawal limitations, the holder of the NFT can withdraw at any moment.

3. Invite 2 Earn

Low barriers to entry to gain references: To acquire referrals, all that is required of you is to give your link to a family, friend, or even just to a stranger you meet on social networks. You will then earn an immediate reward, which will be in your wallet and ready for withdrawal when the user has completed the last step of joining the site, which is the purchase of their own NFT.

4. The system is Independent and self-governed

Blockchain technology, on which the algorithm is built, provides a risk-free and secure environment for the trading of assets. It does not need any further activities for intervention and enables you to create income without allowing access to other users' assets, so providing security for all users of the platform.

FAQ

  • How much credit will an existing member get for bringing in new customers?

    For even referral in the first cycle, you will get 20% for each referral. In accordance your referral will get 20% from his referrals in the first cycle, but will get another 10% for each referral your referral brings and the same is for the third cycle which will reward you with a 5% reward but the whole system still gets the same % per cycle as was explained above.

  • I'm interested in purchasing multiple NFTs, can I?

    Yes, You Can!

  • If I were to purchase an NFT and then give it as a present to someone else, would the new owner be able to use it immediately to take part in the referral system that was built on it already?

    Like any asset on the blockchain, the NFT can be passed around BUT! The referral system is tied to your wallet; thus, the present will include only the NFT with no referrals on it!

  • How many referrals I can add to my tree /or trees?

    There is no limit on how many referrals can be behind you! Neither behind your first second and third-step referrals! There is a possibility to build huge nets of passive income together with the Exodus algorithm!

  • Will the NFT, which currently is in my wallet, also can be put up for sale?

    Yes of course but there is no logic in doing so, better go and get some referrals!

  • When can I withdraw my rewards for bringing referrals?

    Whenever you will withdraw you can! There are NO FEES from our side!

  • Is it possible to get freaking rich with Exodus?

    We bet you can!

  • Is there a possibility for the project to block the withdrawal function?

    No, such a thing cannot happen since the smart contract is deployed and cannot be changed along the way.

  • How the DAO system will work?

    Each NFT grants its holder a vote qualification, with every additional NFT the holder’s number of votes increases. There would be many options for the voters to choose from and all options will be discussed after the first round of sales.

  • Is there a possibility that I can withdraw more than I spent initially?

    Of course! This is the concept of the project.

  • Can I use another wallet rather than using Metamask?

    You can use any wallet that has integration with the Binance Smart Chain and is eligible for usage with Web 3 Dapps.

  • When the Exodus Card will be released?

    TBA, the Exodus Card is under development and will be released as soon as possible but only after all tests and verifications have been conducted.
